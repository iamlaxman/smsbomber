Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SMS Bomber Nepal ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and SMS bombing tool.

By using SMS Bomber Nepal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect NO personal data. SMS Bomber Nepal is a fully client-side application. Here is what you should know:

  • Phone Numbers: The phone numbers you enter are sent directly from your browser to third-party API providers. We do not store, log, or transmit phone numbers to our own servers.
  • No Accounts: We do not require registration, login, or any form of user account.
  • No Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or any form of persistent storage for tracking purposes.
  • No IP Logging: We do not maintain server logs of IP addresses or request data.
Key Point: This tool runs entirely in your browser. All API calls are made directly from your device to third-party services. We never see, store, or process your data.

Third-Party APIs

Our tool communicates directly with the following third-party API providers:

  • ClampHook — backend.clamphook.com
  • Ambition Guru — api.ambition.guru
  • MeroDoctor — merodoctor.com

When you send an SMS, your phone number is transmitted to these third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

We are not responsible for how these third-party services handle your data.

Data Storage

We store nothing. SMS Bomber Nepal is a static website with no backend database. All processing happens locally in your browser. Specifically:

  • No phone numbers are saved to any server
  • No message logs are retained
  • No user sessions are maintained
  • No analytics or usage data is collected
  • The tool functions completely offline after the initial page load

Security

While we implement a client-side only architecture to minimize data exposure, please be aware that:

  • All API calls are made over HTTPS (encrypted)
  • Your phone number is transmitted to third-party APIs
  • We cannot guarantee the security of data processed by third-party services
  • Use of public WiFi networks may expose your traffic to others

Children's Privacy

Our tool is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this tool, please be aware that no data is stored or retained by us.

Legal Compliance

This tool is provided for educational purposes only. Users are solely responsible for complying with all applicable laws, including but not limited to:

  • Nepal Electronic Transactions Act 2063
  • Nepal Privacy Act 2075
  • Any local, national, or international laws regarding telecommunications and privacy

We cooperate with law enforcement authorities when required by law. However, since we store no user data, there is typically no information to provide.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of SMS Bomber Nepal after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

Contact
